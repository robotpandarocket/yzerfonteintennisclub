Yzerfontein Tennis Courts

Visitor Rules & Information

These courts are managed by the Yzerfontein Tennis Club, a local volunteer-run club. While membership offers regular access and supports the upkeep of the courts, visitors are also welcome to book and play. All levels of play are encouraged - whether you're new to the game or a regular player.

Saturday Social Tennis

Informal, friendly tennis - all welcome. No booking needed. Just bring your racquet and join in.
  • When: Saturdays, 09:30-11:30 (weather permitting)
  • Members: R30 per session (ball fee)
  • Guests: R60 per session (includes R30 guest fee + R30 ball fee)

Court Hours

Courts are open daily, weather permitting:
  • May-Oct: 08:00-19:00
  • Nov-Apr: 07:00-20:00
Visitor access is through the Court 2 gate (left-hand side) - key available from the Yzerfontein Sports & Recreational Centre.

Court Fees & Bookings

Member Court Use (Outside Social Tennis)
  • Members: Free of charge
  • Guests playing with a member: R30 per guest per session
Court Bookings (Non-Members, Outside Social Sessions)
  • 2 Players (2 hours): R100
  • 4 Players (2 hours): R200
  • Refundable key deposit: R100
Bookings depend on availability, with preference given to members. To book and pay, visit the Yzerfontein Sports & Recreational Centre (Cnr Buitekant & Beach Rd) or call 022 451 2819.

Court Rules

While You're on Court
Help keep the courts in good condition for everyone:
  • Wear non-marking tennis shoes only
  • Use the soft broom provided to sweep away leaves, sand, or moisture before play
  • Lock the gate after your session
What Not to Do on Court
These actions damage the courts and affect everyone's use:
  • Do not play if the surface is wet or damp (including early morning dew)
  • Do not use the fence to warm up - please use the hitting wall
  • Do not bring bikes, skateboards or scooters onto the court
  • Do not wear shoes with studs, spikes, or hard soles - only tennis shoes allowed
  • Do not drag chairs, coolers, or heavy items across the court
  • Do not drive or park any vehicles on the courts
Parents and guardians are responsible for ensuring their children follow these rules. No children under 12 unsupervised.

Baanreëls

Terwyl Jy op die Baan is
Help om die bane in goeie toestand te hou vir almal:
  • Dra slegs tennisskoene met nie-merkende soles
  • Gebruik die sagte besem om blare, sand of vog voor spel te verwyder
  • Sluit die hek ná jou sessie
Wat Nie Toegelaat Word Nie
Hierdie aksies beskadig die bane en beïnvloed almal se spel:
  • Moenie speel as die baan nat of klam is nie (insluitend oggenddou)
  • Moenie die heining gebruik om op te warm nie - gebruik die slaanmuur
  • Moenie fietse, skaatsplanke of skutters op die baan bring nie
  • Moenie skoene met penne, spykers of harde soles dra nie - net tennisskoene
  • Moenie stoele, koelboksies of swaar items oor die baan sleep nie
  • Moenie voertuie op die bane bestuur of parkeer nie
Ouers is verantwoordelik dat kinders die reëls volg. Geen kinders onder 12 sonder toesig nie.
