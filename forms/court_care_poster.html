DO NOT PLAY ON WET COURTS
Wet courts are slippery and dangerous
  • Do not play if the surface is wet or damp
  • Check the shaded area behind the baseline - it stays wet longer
  • Moenie speel as die oppervlak nat of klam is nie
  • Kyk na die skaduwee area agter die baselyn - dit bly langer nat
