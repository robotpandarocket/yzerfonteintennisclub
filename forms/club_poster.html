Yzerfontein Social Tennis Club

Keeping tennis thriving in Yzerfontein for the long term

Yzerfontein Tennis Club was formally established in September 2022 by a group of local players who wanted to make tennis more accessible, enjoyable, and part of daily life in our town. What began with just ten members has grown into a small but active club, open to both residents and visitors.

We don't aim to be a large or competitive club. Our focus is on creating a space that's easy to be part of, welcoming to all, and simple to keep running for the long term. Whether you play regularly or just now and then, you're invited to take part â€” especially if you're new to tennis, new to town, or returning to the game after some time away.

In July 2025, our courts were officially reopened after a full resurfacing. This upgrade was made possible through the hard work of the previous committee, the support of our members, and a partnership with the Yzerfontein Sport and Recreation Club and the local municipality. The new courts have already brought new energy to the club and are an asset we're proud to look after.

We support weekly social tennis, encourage casual and private play, and welcome visitors while looking after the needs of our local members. Membership fees are kept as low as possible to keep tennis accessible to everyone.

Court Access and Membership

Our courts are available for both members and visitors, with bookings handled through the Yzerfontein Sport and Recreation Club.

By becoming a member, you enjoy free court access, reduced social tennis fees and have access to all club events.

It's a great way to meet people, stay active, and be part of our growing tennis community.
