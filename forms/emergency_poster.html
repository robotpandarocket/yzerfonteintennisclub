What to Do in an Emergency

  • Call the relevant emergency service above
  • Clearly state your location (see above)
  • Describe the emergency: what happened, how many injured, their condition
  • Stay calm and send someone to meet emergency responders at the street entrance

If Someone is Injured

  • Unconscious but breathing: Place in recovery position
  • Not breathing: Start CPR immediately
  • Heavy bleeding: Apply firm pressure with cloth or clothing
