Important Notes
  • Membership of the Yzerfontein Sport and Recreation Club (YSRC) is mandatory. A separate application form must be completed (see attached). You may include the YSRC fee with your YTC payment; the YTC Treasurer will transfer the amount to YSRC on your behalf.
  • Membership fees apply to the full 2025/2026 season (1 March 2025 to 28 February 2026). Fees are fixed for the season; no pro-rata rates will be offered.
  • Children under 12 may not play without adult supervision.
  • All members and guests must adhere to club rules.
